About

Big Blue, the organizers of BUST, The Melee Games, New Game Plus, and Smash on the Hill, present a new major Smash tournament series, Shine!

Feature games include Smash 64, Melee, and Wii U, with Singles and Doubles brackets for each.

In addition, we are running a Pokken Tournament bracket, and an open 5v5 crew battle bracket for Melee and Wii U! Sign up with your friends and represent your crew at this very unique event!

Venue

Shine 2016: Stay In Your Lane will be held at the Seaport World Trade Center. The over 30,000 sq. ft. venue overlooks historic Boston harbor, and is just minutes away from the airport and downtown Boston! The MBTA Silver Line Waterfront (SL1) provides free service from Logan International Airport terminals to the WTC Station.
